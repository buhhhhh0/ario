Hiring Intelligence FAQs

Quick answers for teams evaluating interview capture, privacy, and safe rollout.

What should interview intelligence do well? +

It should capture accurate transcripts, separate speakers, surface decision signals, and fit into the hiring tools your team already uses.

Can Lantern Ario start with a small team? +

Teams can start with a lightweight setup and expand as hiring workflows grow. Final pricing depends on workspace size and rollout needs.

Can we use offline interview recordings? +

Yes. Offline interview recordings can be turned into summaries, follow-ups, and searchable recruiting memory when uploaded securely.

How private are interview records? +

Lantern Ario is designed around controlled access, retention settings, and enterprise security expectations for hiring conversations.

Is interview recording legal and safe? +

They can be, when teams follow consent, recording, retention, and data-protection requirements for their region and interview process.

What risks should hiring teams manage? +

The main risks are inaccurate summaries, unclear consent, and poor access control. Use source-backed notes and clear team policies to reduce those risks.

Can Lantern Ario support more than interviews? +

Yes. Teams can use Lantern Ario for intake calls, interview loops, debriefs, memory search, scorecards, and ATS sync.
